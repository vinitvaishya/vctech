Organizations today, are looking to immensely benefit from AI-based Video Analytics and are aggressively accepting these solutions to make their physical places smarter & safer. We aim to provide immense operational benefits to our customers which not only helps them reduce their expense immensely but at the same time increases their efficiency tremendously.

How does Video Analytics work

We are committed to bringing products which are easy to connect & simple to use, we hereby present SMART BOX a simple plug & play device which once connected to your Cameras, will make them intelligent. Smart BOX is having wide application areas from as small as ATM’s to as big as Manufacturing plant, Smart BOX stands with our principles of creating Values for our customers by becoming better & better every day. Smart BOX does the same thing. This is a pure Make in India product, which has been designed, developed & manufactured here in India. It is extremely easy to install and very Simple to use device.

Activities performed by Smart BOX:

Smart BOX makes Cameras intelligent by using their Video footage to perform AI-based Video Analytics. This has immense operational benefits:
  • Facial Recognition
  • Customer Demography
  • People Counting
  • Entry Restrictions
  • SOP Deviations

We can make your existing cameras recognize the face of your employees as well as visitors whoever is coming in or going out of your premises. Many organizations world over are using this technology to identify their employees & customers who frequently visit their premises. It has helped the organizations to mark contactless attendance of their employees and also to keep a track of employees who frequently move out during office hours which usually goes unaccounted in any organization. To get an immediate alert if any VIP customer or any Blacklist Customer arrives in their premise. This we do by connecting your existing Entry/Exit Cameras to Smart BOX which helps in performing Facial Recognition. This can be immensely beneficial for Retail Outlets, Bank Branches, Educational Institutions, Corporate Offices, or any organizations which are interested to know who is coming in & going out of their premises.

In retail, it is extremely important to know your customer & if you know their demography, you can design your marketing campaigns specifically for the target audience thus helping you increase your Revenues. Now how do we do this? We connect your existing CCTV Camera to our Smart BOX which then analyses the footage on a real-time basis to provide you with Age & Gender Analysis. This can be immensely beneficial for Retail Outlets, QSRs or any organizations who want to know age and gender of customers who visit their premises

By connecting your existing Entry/Exit Camera to Smart BOX, you can come to know exactly how many people have entered or left your premises or how many are currently inside your premises. This can be extremely beneficial for various organizations that want to track the Footfall Count per Store. For Retail Outlets, they can use this data to get store conversion ratio by simply dividing the total into people with the total number of Sale which will give your conversion ratio. Immensely beneficial for Retail Outlets, QSRs, Bank branches, Educational Institutions.

In the -post-COVID times, this can be extremely beneficial for organizations that want to restrict entry depending upon the total number of people inside their premises. As the government has made compulsory on the total percentage of the crowd that is acceptable in different types of premises, this can easily help organizations to manage the total amount of people by doing people counting & helping them in real-time crowd management. Immensely beneficial for Retail Outlets, Salons, QSRs, Bank branches, Educational Institutions.

By connecting your existing Cameras to Smart BOX, you can track various SOP deviations like POS Manned/Unmanned inside Retail Outlets, Store Opening/Closing, Wearing of Uniform by employees inside the premises, following certain SOP’s for certain critical tasks, Wearing safety harnesses, etc. Smart BOX can make your cameras smarter, efficient & reliable to increase your productivity with the help of technology.
